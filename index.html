1. What are the main differences between Web1, Web2, and Web3? (Click to Show)

  • Web1: Basic websites. You can only read information, not change it.
  • Web2: Interactive websites. You can share and create content, like on social media.
  • Web3: Websites use blockchain. You control your data. Secure deals with smart contracts.
